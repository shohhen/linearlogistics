Since beginning

At Linestar Logistics, we wouldn’t be where we are today if it wasn’t for the dedicated team that works night and day for us driving across the country. We’re proud to say that most of our drivers who have been with us since the beginning are still with us today. With our founder originally being a trucker himself, we know how important it is for a driver’s rights to be understood.

$3700-$9300 weekly

That’s why, if you drive with us, you’re treated like family, provided with job security, a competitive salary, and the latest freight vehicles. To be more specific, our drivers are earning between $3700-$9300 weekly. At the minimum, they’re earning $3200 without any commission or bonuses. Most importantly, we pay you on time, cover your layovers and detention too.

We prioritize our drivers

We also understand how tiring long haul driving can be. For this reason, we prioritize our driver’s comfort and safety by using the latest Freightliners, Volvos, Kenworth, and International’s well before their commercial release.

24/7 specialist logistics team

We know first hand the risks, burdens, and time delays drivers can face, which is why we equip our vehicles with Samsara devices, with forward facing cameras, E logs, Transponders for tolls and scales, GPS, and more. Most importantly, we have a 24/7 specialist logistics team who monitor your vehicle and are there to help you with any problem or reload at any time you need.

Driver qualification

At the minimum, our drivers must have the following:

  • Aged 23 years old or above
  • Own a Valid CDL-A License
  • A minimum of 12 months of current trucker/trailer experience
  • No more than 6 jobs in the last three years
  • No more than 2 out of service violations in n the last two years
  • No DUI, Test Refusal, DWI, Drug Convictions, Reckless Driving
  • No additional felonies in the past five years, including Hit and Run, Leaving the scene of an accident, Passing a School Bus, or Racing.
  • You must have passed and had a current DOT physical.
  • Willing to take and pass DOT drug tests
